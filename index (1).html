About Us

Brew & Bloom Cafe was started in 2018 by Priya and Arjun Sharma. What started as a small tea stall in Gaya slowly grew into a full cafe serving coffee, snacks, and baked goods.

We believe good coffee and good company go hand in hand. Our beans are sourced from Coorg and we bake fresh every morning. Every Friday evening, we also have a small live music session for our regulars.
